United Arab Emirates Traditions

Falconry is one of the most traditional sports in the United Arab Emirates and has been a tradition for more than 2,000 years. It is still being passed down from generation to generation to keep the tradition alive. It is said that the requirement to be able to control a falcon is to have patience and bond with the bird, so that the both of you can trust each other. For some time now falconers have been able to breed falcons and produce new techniques to manage a falcon. The UAE has a falcon experience that gives you an unimaginable experience of an ancient tradition in person. Falconry was first used as a method for hunting in the past, nowadays falconers breed and train their falcons. But they occasionally take their falcons to exhibitions and events that are taking place all year long in UAE. The national bird of UAE is the falcon, which is the official emblem of the country that consists of a golden falcon.
